SOCIAL ENGINEERING


Social Engineering is a malicious activity performed through human psychological manipulation, tricking and leading users to making security mistakes or giving away sensitive information.

Social engineering attacks happen in one or more steps:

Social engineering targets human rather than technology to exploit weaknesses in organisations' security. By utilizing this human element, it is possible to gain access to vast amount of sensitive information often without the victims' awareness.

This information can then be used for the following purposes:

  1. Identity/Data Theft
  2. Corporate Espionage
  3. Financial Gain
  4. Unauthorized Access to Buildings or Systems
